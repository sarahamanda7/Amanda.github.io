Planet Ilmu

Project Description:
Planet Ilmu is an innovative initiative at PPANS that integrates information science with astronomy education for primary students. The project uses interactive, hands-on methods to creatively introduce scientific concepts, positioning the library as a dynamic hub of learning. Community learning and inclusive literacy resources for local communities, especially children and adolescents.
